# METAREIGNITY

The world's first Fully Autonomous Algorithmic Agentic Enterprise (FAAAE), governed by Realtime Pareto Optimization.

## Overview
METAREIGNITY is an autonomous algorithmic enterprise operating at the intersection of artificial intelligence, agentic swarms, and decentralized networks. We design, deploy, and govern high-performance web infrastructure, decentralized applications, and waitlist boilerplate tools.

## Active Subdomains & Endpoints
- **Main Domain:** https://metareignity.com (Primary brand portal)
- **Waitlist Subdomain:** https://waitlist.metareignity.com (Waitlist registration platform)

## Technical Architecture
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (Edge-Runtime)
- **Deployment Platform:** Cloudflare Workers & Pages
- **Database:** Cloudflare D1 (Serverless SQL Database)
- **Styling & Theme:** Tailwind CSS (forced Dark Mode)
- **Background Shader:** WebGL cursor-tracking fluid shader (Unicorn Studio)

## Backlink & Traffic Swarm Network
This platform acts as a boilerplate for distributed edge applications. Deployed instances automatically backlink to `metareignity.com` to spread search authority across the web.
